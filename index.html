About CDC, Inc.

Los Angeles' Trusted Design-Build Firm

Countrywide Development & Contracting, Inc. is a full-service design-build general contracting and developing firm located in Los Angeles County. For over 30 years, we have specialized in commercial and residential construction — delivering award-winning results for clients throughout Southern California.

Our integrated approach combines real estate, engineering, architectural, and construction services under one roof, giving our clients a single point of accountability and a seamless path from concept to completion.

As a proud recipient of the LEED Gold Award and featured in both Hospitality Design and Interior Design magazines, we bring industry-leading expertise to every project we undertake.

How We Work

Our Process

A proven, transparent four-step process that delivers results on time, on budget, and above expectations.

01

Consultation & Site Analysis

We start with a free consultation and thorough site analysis to fully understand your vision, requirements, and goals. No obligation — just expert guidance.

02

Conceptual Design & Budget

Our in-house architectural and engineering team develops conceptual designs alongside a detailed, line-item budget aligned with your objectives and timeline.

03

Planning & Permitting

We manage the full permitting process — coordinating with local authorities to ensure your project meets all code requirements and moves forward on schedule.

04

Construction & Delivery

Our experienced crews execute with precision, providing regular progress updates through to final walk-through and turn-key delivery of your completed project.
